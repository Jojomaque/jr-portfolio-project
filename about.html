I am Jojo Russell

Front-end developer based in Edinburgh

In 2022, a life-changing event--a new baby--- inspired me to pivot from the world of accounting to the dynamic realm of tech and web design & development. This marked the beginning of an exciting journey towards building immersive digital experiences.
-- Educational Persuits --
I delved into the world of UX and UI through a transformative three-month bootcamp in partnership with code institute and Coventry University. This intensive program honed my design skills and instilled a deep understanding of user-centric principles.

Infused with boundless crativity, I am eager for the next chapter in my tech journey. Each project presents an opportunity to craft impactful digital solutions and push the boundries of inovation. I am poised to weave my unique blend of design flair and technical accumen into every venture.
